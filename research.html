National

Nationally, the number of high school graduates is expected to peak in the mid-2020s before entering a period of modest decline through the end of the projections in 2037.

Regional

Arizona high school graduates are projected to peak in 2026 at 85,030 graduates. Between 2018 and 2037, high school graduates are projected to average 78,820 per year.

Data Source
Arizona High School Graduate Projections

Reference: Western Interstate Commission for Higher Education, Knocking at the College Door: Projections of High School Graduates, 2020, www.knocking.wiche.edu.
Specific to Arizona: Public school graduates for SY 2013-14 to 2018-19 are ESTIMATES COMPUTED BY WICHE (not confirmed total annual graduates) using data about graduates from ninth-grade cohorts that WICHE was pointed to at https://www.azed.gov/accountability-research/data/
